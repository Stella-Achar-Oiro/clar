# What is CLAR?

CLAR (Clinical Lab Analysis & Reporting) is a web application that helps patients understand their medical reports.

Upload a lab result, radiology report, or discharge summary and CLAR will:

- Translate findings into plain English
- Flag anything that needs attention and explain why
- Suggest questions to bring to your next doctor's appointment
- Let you ask follow-up questions in a conversational chat

CLAR is not a diagnostic tool. It does not replace your doctor. It is designed to help you arrive at appointments informed and with better questions.

## Who is it for?

CLAR is for patients who receive medical documents and want to understand what they say before — or after — speaking with a clinician.

## How it works

1. You sign in and upload a PDF or image of your report.
2. CLAR removes identifying information before any analysis begins.
3. The de-identified text is analysed and structured findings are returned.
4. You can ask follow-up questions in the chat panel, grounded in your specific report.

All processing happens server-side. Your original document is never stored.
