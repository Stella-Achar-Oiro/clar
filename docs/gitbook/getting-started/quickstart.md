# Quickstart

## Sign in

CLAR uses secure sign-in via [Clerk](https://clerk.com). Click **Sign in to get started** on the home page and authenticate with your Google account or email.

## Upload a report

Once signed in, you will see the upload area. You can:

- Drag and drop a PDF or image file onto the drop zone
- Click the drop zone to open a file picker
- Use **Try a sample report** to see CLAR in action without uploading your own document

Supported formats: PDF, PNG, JPG, JPEG, TIFF (max 10 MB).

## Read your results

After a few seconds CLAR returns three views, accessible from the left sidebar:

| View | What you see |
|---|---|
| **Findings** | Every result from your report explained in plain English |
| **Urgency Summary** | Only findings that may need timely attention |
| **Doctor Questions** | Suggested questions to bring to your next appointment |

## Ask CLAR

Click **Ask CLAR** in the sidebar to open the chat panel. You can ask anything about your specific report — CLAR will answer using only the information in that document.

Examples:
- "Is my cholesterol level dangerous?"
- "What does 'borderline' mean for my glucose?"
- "Should I be worried about anything here?"

## Upload another report

Click **+ New Report** in the top navigation bar to clear the current results and upload a new document.
