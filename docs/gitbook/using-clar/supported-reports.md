# Supported Report Types

CLAR can analyse three categories of medical document:

## Lab Results

Standard blood panels, urine analysis, metabolic panels, lipid panels, thyroid function tests, and similar documents that list test names, measured values, and reference ranges.

CLAR identifies which values are within range, which are outside range, and explains what each measurement means in everyday language.

## Radiology Reports

Written summaries from X-ray, CT, MRI, or ultrasound studies. These are the text reports produced by a radiologist — not the images themselves.

CLAR picks out the key findings and impression, and translates radiological terminology into plain English.

## Discharge Summaries

Documents given to patients when leaving a hospital or emergency department, summarising the diagnosis, treatments given, medications prescribed, and follow-up instructions.

CLAR highlights the most important action items and explains any medical terms used.

---

If your document does not fall neatly into one of these categories, CLAR will still attempt to analyse it and will indicate the detected report type in the sidebar.
