# Asking CLAR Questions

The chat panel lets you ask questions about your report in plain English. CLAR answers using only the content of the report you uploaded — it does not have access to any other medical records or general health databases.

## Opening chat

Click **Ask CLAR** in the left sidebar. On mobile, the chat panel slides up from the bottom of the screen.

## Starter questions

When you open chat, CLAR suggests a few questions based on your specific results. Tap any of them to send it immediately, or type your own question.

## What CLAR can answer

- Explanations of specific values or findings in your report
- What a medical term in the report means
- Whether a particular result is high, low, or normal
- What the urgency level of a finding means in practical terms
- Suggestions for what to discuss with your doctor

## What CLAR cannot answer

- Questions not grounded in your uploaded report
- Diagnostic questions ("Do I have diabetes?")
- Treatment or medication advice
- Questions about reports you haven't uploaded in this session

## Closing chat

Click the **X** in the top-right corner of the chat panel, or tap outside the panel on desktop.
